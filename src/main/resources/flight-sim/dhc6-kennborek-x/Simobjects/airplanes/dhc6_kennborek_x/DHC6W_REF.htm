Turn on Tool Tips

To raise your seat higher press SHIFT-ENTER

Shift E operates the Pax/Cargo Doors

Shift E +2 will operate the Pilot Doors

If using AUTOSTART.... Check to make sure the Generators are switched to ON in the Overhead Panel

The RED Button on the left / right grips of the VC
Yokes will open the ATC dialog box.

To hear the Exit Door sounds....It has to be quiet in the cockpit Do it before starting engines or after Engine Shutdown

For Best Frame Rates...Keep Windows Closed
